Shekhawati: Heritage & Spiritual Gallery

Discover the timeless charm of Shekhawati through our gallery, where heritage, art, and spirituality come together. From beautifully painted havelis and historic forts to sacred temples and vibrant cultural moments, these images capture the true spirit of this remarkable region of Rajasthan.

Explore Shekhawati Through Images

Mandawa Haveli fresco paintings

Intricate fresco paintings that decorate the walls of Mandawa havelis, showcasing the artistic brilliance of Shekhawati merchants.

Podar Haveli heritage museum

A beautifully restored haveli museum displaying traditional Shekhawati art, architecture, and cultural heritage.

Shekhawati haveli wall murals

Colorful murals depicting mythology, royal life, and historical stories that make Shekhawati famous as an open-air art gallery.

Morarka Haveli architecture

A magnificent haveli known for its grand courtyards, carved balconies, and detailed fresco artwork.

Khatu Shyam Ji Temple

A sacred pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Shyam, attracting thousands of devotees from across India.

Salasar Balaji Temple

One of Rajasthan’s most revered Hanuman temples, known for its spiritual atmosphere and devotion.

Jeen Mata Temple

An ancient temple nestled in the Aravalli hills, dedicated to Goddess Jeen Mata.

Rani Sati Temple

A famous temple in Jhunjhunu known for its marble architecture and spiritual significance.

Mandawa Fort courtyard

The royal courtyard of Mandawa Fort reflecting the grandeur and lifestyle of Rajput rulers.

Dundlod Fort heritage hotel

A historic fort transformed into a heritage hotel offering a glimpse of royal Rajasthani living.

Mukundgarh Fort palace

A majestic palace showcasing Rajput architecture and the royal heritage of Mukundgarh.

Mandawa Fort interior

The richly decorated interiors of Mandawa Fort filled with antique furniture and traditional artwork.

Rajasthani folk dance

Energetic traditional dance performances reflecting the vibrant culture of Rajasthan.

Shekhawati cultural festival

A colorful festival celebrating the region’s folk music, dance, crafts, and heritage.

Local bazaar scene

Busy local markets where visitors can explore handicrafts, textiles, and traditional Rajasthani goods.

Traditional Rajasthani attire

Bright and beautifully embroidered garments representing the unique identity of Rajasthan.

Mandawa town view

A scenic view of Mandawa town known for its historic havelis and charming streets.

Nawalgarh heritage streets

Streets lined with beautifully painted havelis that reflect the rich heritage of Nawalgarh.

Tal Chhapar Sanctuary

Located in the Shekhawati region of Rajasthan, Tal Chhapar Sanctuary is famous for its open grasslands and large population of blackbucks.

Shekhawati village landscape

A peaceful rural landscape showcasing the desert beauty and traditional lifestyle of Shekhawati.

Shekhawati aerial town view

An aerial glimpse of Shekhawati’s towns surrounded by desert terrain and historic architecture.
